The Best Songs According to Sanjeevani

My Top Favourite Evergreen Songs

I generally like romantic songs and the songs which beautify each line due to their composition. So, my favourite songs are based on this theme only.

1. Yeh Raatein Yeh Mausam

This song is from the movie named "Dilli Ka Thug". This song is sung by "The Queen of Indipop" who is ASHA BHOSALE. I really liked each and every word of the song and the singers have poured the soul in the song too. This song is also about embracing the beauty of nature.

You can click this link to listen: Yeh Raatein Yeh Mausam

2. Dekha Hazaro Dafaa

This song is from the movie named "Rustom". This song is sung by Arijit Singh. I really love his voice, especially the romantic song by him are just a wow..! The every word of this song directly gives the feeling as the singer sings.

You can click this link to listen: Dekha Hazaro Dafaa

3. Sun Raha Hai Na Tu Female Version

This song is from the movie named "Ashiqui 2". This song is sung by Shreya Ghoshal. Her voice is so pleasant and peaceful. The song is having so much feeling only through the words and because of singer. I loved the song.

You can click this link to listen: Sun Raha Hai Na Tu Female Version

4. Jaan Nisaar

This song is from the movie named "Kedarnath". This song is sung by Arijit Singh. This song is also close to my heart, Don't know why? But, just loved it!

You can click this link to listen: Jaan Nisaar

5. Naina

This song is from the movie named "Dangal". This song is too sung by Arijit Singh. This song tells the challenges faced by people when they move to other place especially from small town to bigger one.

You can click this link to listen: Naina
